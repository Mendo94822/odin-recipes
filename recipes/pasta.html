College Student Pasta

Image taken from google of how the pasta should look like, but never actually does. The cheese and spinach in the photo are not in this recipe btw.

This is a 3 ingredient recipe adding up to a total of 1.02$ per portion. (Well, at least that's the amount I get the ingredients for lol)

Ingredients:
  • Pasta, (duh) specifically spaghetti.
  • Canned tomatoes. (Normal ones are more expensive where I live lol)
  • Minced Beef.

  1. Boil water in a pot.
  2. Add Spaghetti to the pot.
  3. Spaguetti go brrrr
  4. While the spaghetti cooks, blend the tomatoes and add them to a pan with the meat in low heat.
  5. When the spaghetti is done after 4 min, add it to the same pan with the meat and tomatoes and change the heat to medium.
  6. Add your favorite seasonings and enjoy.
  7. Repeat every day because pasta is the best thing that exists.
